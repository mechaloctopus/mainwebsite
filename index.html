About Us

Welcome to Farmhouse Mushroom Company, nestled in Bokchito, Oklahoma. Our journey began with a deep connection to nature and a passion for mushrooms. Josh—who has over 10 years of hands-on experience—has mastered the art of tuning into each mushroom's spirit to cultivate pristine fungi. Our commitment to quality, community, and sustainability fuels our every harvest.

From rustic beginnings to modern practices, we proudly celebrate the profound health benefits and culinary delights of mushrooms.
