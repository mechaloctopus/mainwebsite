About Our Farmhouse

Welcome to our charming farmhouse nestled in the heart of Bokchito, Oklahoma. At Farmhouse Mushroom Company, we honor time-honored traditions and embrace the simple joys of country living as we cultivate our mushrooms with care.

Experience the warmth of a close-knit community, where every harvest celebrates nature, hard work, and genuine down-home hospitality. Our farmhouse exudes old-time values and authentic country charm in every step.
