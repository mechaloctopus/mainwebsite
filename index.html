About Us

Located in Bokchito, Oklahoma, Farmhouse Mushroom Company began with a deep connection to nature and a passion for mushrooms. Josh, with over 10 years of experience, tunes into the spirit of every mushroom to cultivate pristine fungi. Recognizing the profound health benefits and culinary delights of mushrooms, we embraced sustainable farmhouse practices to grow an array of culinary mushrooms.

Our commitment to quality, community, and sustainability shines through every harvest. We invite you to explore our story and meet the team that makes it all possible.
